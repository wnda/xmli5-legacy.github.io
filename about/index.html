About XMLi5

XMLi5 Ltd. is a Software house who specialise in the provision of enterprise level ASP.NET eCommerce solutions to the IBM SMBE Market. Although small, the staff members of XMLi5 are highly experienced Consultants/Technicians with, on average, some 25 years experience each in the IBM Midrange arena.

XMLi5 Ltd. was formed in August 2005 by Technical Director Maurice O’Prey. Maurice has a long history in the development of Software Systems beginning his career in 1981 on the IBM System/38. After working for several major Software Houses he started his own Company to market and sell eCommerce Systems specifically designed to integrate Microsoft .NET with the IBM iSeries (AS/400), now named the IBM i5.

For the past 10 years Maurice O’Prey has worked on the development and implementation of many eCommerce and XML trading Systems for many organisations (large and Small).

As well as strong Technical skills O’Prey has Project Managed (and implemented successfully) large Retail & Distribution Projects, both here in the UK, Europe and the USA. Projects managed by O’Prey have ranged from £25K to £2 Million.

XMLi5 Ltd. is a Technical Leader in the area of interfacing the Microsoft .NET platform with the IBM i5 (iSeries, AS/400). The Company is an IBM Business Partner in Development and a Microsoft Registered Business Partner.

The XMLi5 product is 100% developed by, and is 100% owned by, XMLi5 Ltd. The Company’s aim is to continually extend the product, offering a stable implementation platform and enhanced functionality, to Customer’s for many years to come.

Our mission is to grow a base of ‘Satisfied’ Customers.
